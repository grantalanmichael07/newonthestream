02The Film

The river taught him to stand still.

On The Stream is a short documentary about Al White, an Indiana fly fisherman whose time on the river becomes a reflection on life. After a turbulent youth, Al finds peace in fly fishing and discovers that the water can be a place of healing.

Along the way he builds community in unexpected places and encourages others to find their own path to peace.
